2022 Colt License Authorized M733 GBB Airsoft
VFC Colt Model 733 Commando GBBR is built under authorized by Cybergun, with fully authorized markings. Reproduce The Model 733 Commando classic type, including details and engravings are complete and exquisite.
Classic Details Of The M733 Are Fully Presented

This product styling is taken from the Model 733 Commando of the real firearms manufacturer - Colt. It includes a 11.5-inch outer barrel, a CAR-15 style front sight, a special CAR-15 skinny round carbine handguard, and a carrying handle upper receiver, CAR-15 late-type dummy forward assist knob, CAR-15 early-type polymer buttstock, etc... plus the lower receiver with a fully authorized Colt M16A2 marking, all the classic appearance details of the Colt M733 are integrated in one place.

Improved Lower Receiver Internal Structure

the GBBR is not only directly put into the new design "Non-cut lever" Steel Hammer Set, but also improve the structure of the lower receiver, increase the fitting depth, and the structure of the firing pin base parts is greatly simplified, making the valve knocking action more stable, the durability of the parts is effectively improved, and the maintenance will be easier. (This new lower receiver can be directly assembled with previous VFC M4 GBBR upper receiver.)

Improved Internal Structure Improve Performance In One Piece

The VFC M733 GBBR this time has not only got the authorization from Colt, but also insert all update mechanism of VFC AR GBB system into it at one time, including the new improved Lower Receiver Internal structure, the improvement of the firing pin design and newly designed steel "Non-cut lever" steel hammer set, this time added a new setting buffer spring, with our new G.I. style gas magazine, it will definitely improve the overall performance of the M733!
